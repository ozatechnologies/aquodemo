About Aqizo

Aqizo International

Aqizo International is a trusted name in the global export of premium Indian rice and spices. Based in India, we specialize in delivering the finest basmati rice, non-basmati rice, and a wide range of authentic spices including chilli, coriander, cumin, sesame, turmeric, and more to international markets.

With a strong commitment to purity, flavor, and freshness, every product we supply reflects the rich heritage and taste of Indian agriculture. Our focus on uncompromising quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction makes Aqizo International a reliable partner for premium agro-products and spice exports worldwide.
